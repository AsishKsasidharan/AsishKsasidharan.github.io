Let’s Explore the Ocean

About Me

I am an Ocean Science enthusiast specializing in physical oceanography, machine learning, and ocean observations. Currently, I work as a Senior Research Fellow at INCOIS, Hyderabad in the Ocean Observation Network Group.

My research focuses on freshwater intrusion, ocean mixing, and salinity variability in the Bay of Bengal, integrating remote sensing, in-situ observations, and numerical modeling.

I apply unsupervised machine learning techniques such as Gaussian Mixture Models (GMM) to classify oceanographic profiles and understand upper-ocean processes.

I was awarded 1st Prize at OSICON 2025 (Goa) for my poster titled “Unsupervised Classification of Freshwater Profiles in the Bay of Bengal”.

Research Interests
