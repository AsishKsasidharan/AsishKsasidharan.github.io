Exploring the Ocean
Data • Dynamics • Discovery

I am an ocean scientist working at the intersection of physical oceanography, machine learning, and ocean observations. Currently, I serve as a Senior Research Fellow at INCOIS, Hyderabad.

My research focuses on freshwater intrusion, ocean mixing, and salinity variability in the Bay of Bengal, using remote sensing, in-situ observations, and numerical models.

I apply unsupervised machine learning techniques such as Gaussian Mixture Models (GMM) to classify oceanographic profiles.

I received the Best Poster Award at OSICON 2025 (Goa) for my work on unsupervised classification of freshwater profiles.

Research Interests

  • Freshwater intrusion & upper-ocean mixing
  • Salinity variability in the Bay of Bengal
  • Machine learning for oceanographic data
  • Upper-ocean modeling (PWP, KPP)
  • Cyclone impact & observing system design
